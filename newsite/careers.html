THE ROLE

The Global Design Team is seeking a talented UX/UI Designer who shares our passion for creating world-class user experiences. You must have proven skills in conceptual design, interaction design, information architecture and visual design. You will be responsible for all stages of design in a given product area. You must be able to work well with stakeholders, engineers and product managers to be successful at designing simple solutions to highly technical problems. The ideal candidate has proficiency in user-centered design, a love of analytics and knowledge of responsive design patterns.

  • Design UI mocks, site flows, wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes for all screen sizes
  • Complete designs iteratively that meet business goals and requirements while maintaining and thoughtfully refining design patterns and UI standards.
  • Perpetually demonstrate expertise UI/UX design both in discussion and practice.
  • Design and manage style guidelines
  • Be involved in running usability studies on your work and collaborating on early UX research

REQUIRED SKILLS/EXPERIENCE

  • 2-3+ years professional experience in user experience and visual design
  • A portfolio of effective UX/UI design and can talk us through your process
  • A good understanding of Typography, Composition, and Grid Systems
  • Responsive design experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in Graphic design or related. Higher degree a plus
  • Excellent visual design skills with sensitivity to user experience
  • Expert in mobile and desktop optimized design practices
  • Fluent in Adobe creative suite
  • Sketch is our tool of preference
  • Thrive in highly collaborative, fluid, fast-paced open office environment
  • Strong communication skills needed to provide direction and to establish best practices within the team

BONUS POINTS FOR

  • Interest in and experienced with usability and running user testing
  • Experience designing for print
